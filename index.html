How Donavault works
1

Submit your item

Request a prepaid Donavault shipping box. We handle the rest — insured, tracked, and fully documented from the moment it leaves your hands.

2

Expert authentication

Every item is authenticated by certified experts within 48 hours of receipt. Your item receives a Donavault authentication badge and QR-linked certificate.

3

Members bid — exclusively

Your authenticated item goes live to Donavault members only. A 3-day countdown with a fire round in the final 10 minutes drives real competition and higher final prices.

4

You get paid — or give back

Sold: you receive 75% of the final price. Unsold after relist: donate to the Donavault Global Health Foundation and receive a certified tax deduction receipt.

5

Luxury gives back

Donated items are sold by the foundation at accessible prices. One hundred percent of proceeds fund global health initiatives. Your luxury item becomes a force for good.

A portion of every Donavault auction supports the Donavault Global Health Foundation · 501(c)(3) pending · Chicago, Illinois
